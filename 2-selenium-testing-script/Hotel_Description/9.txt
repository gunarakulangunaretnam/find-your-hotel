Amid lush, sprawling gardens, this upscale hotel is 3 km from the ancient Sigiriya Lion Rock fortress and 6 km from the A6 highway.Set around a central garden with lily ponds, the airy, relaxed rooms feature balconies and satellite TV, plus minibars, and tea and coffeemaking facilities.Parking is available. Dining options include a restaurant with open-air seating, plus a bar. There’s also an outdoor pool with a sundeck. Breakfast is extra.
