Surrounded by lush vegetation, this relaxed hotel with an annexe is 8 km from Hurulu Eco park, 9 km from Sigiriya palace and 11 km from exhibits at Sigiriya Museum.Unassuming rooms with tile floors have free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and sitting areas, along with balconies or terraces. Suites add whirlpool tubs. Rooms in the annexe feature views of wildlife.A restaurant with a terrace serves regional and international cuisine. Additional amenities include an outdoor pool with sunloungers, and a spa offering massages, a steam bath and a sauna, as well as gardens with seating
