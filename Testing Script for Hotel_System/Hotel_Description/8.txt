Surrounded by woodland, this unfussy hotel is an 8-minute walk from Dambulla river and 4 km from the Dambulla Cave Temple.Nestled amid greenery, the airy villas come with satellite TV, DVD players, safes and minibars, as well as tea and coffeemaking facilities, balconies and private pools.There are 2 eateries, 1 serving fusion cuisine, plus a poolside cafe and a chic bar. Other amenities include an outdoor pool, tennis, volleyball, archery and horseback riding, as well as a spa with yoga and steam baths.
