Amid tropical gardens off the A6 road, this laid-back hotel is 9 km from the ancient ruins of Sigirya, and 11 km from the historic Golden Temple of Dambulla.The colourful, casual rooms feature TVs and balconies. Upgraded rooms add minibars, and tea and coffeemaking facilities. Room service is available 24/7.Amenities include an outdoor pool and a restaurant. Theres also event space for up to 300 people.
