This relaxed resort is 3 km from Kaludiya Pokuna Forest, 13 km from Sigiriya Lion Rock and 15 km from Dambulla Cave Temple.Airy rooms with wood furnishings and parquet floors offer free Wi-Fi, satellite TVs and terraces, plus safes, minibars, and tea and coffeemakers. Upgraded rooms and suites have living areas and/or Jacuzzis. Room service is available.Amenities consist of an outdoor pool with a kids section, a gym and a game room. Theres also a garden, a gift shop, a lounge, and meeting and event space. Dining options include 3 restaurants (1 is a pizzeria) and 2 bars. A spa offers Thai and Ayurveda treatments. Breakfast is available.
